Offering Count — User Guide

Step-by-step instructions for the counting team

Overview
ℹ️
How it works

Two counters each open the app on their own device and enter quantities independently. Neither can see the other's entries until both have locked their count. The app then compares the two counts side by side and highlights any differences. The offering can only be submitted when both counts fully agree.

⚠️
Important — always physically recount

Do not simply change your number to match the other counter. Pick up the bills again, recount them, and enter what you physically counted. This protects the integrity of the count.

If a check looks incorrect, the confirming counter taps Flag as Wrong. The check turns red and an edit form appears for the original enterer to correct it.

Troubleshooting
📵
Offline banner appears at the top

The app requires internet to sync between devices. Check your Wi-Fi or mobile data connection. Entries made while offline will sync automatically when you reconnect.

🔄
App seems frozen or stuck

Refresh the page. The app will automatically rejoin your session and restore your entries. If both counters are stuck, one counter can tap Reset Session to start the count over, or Clear All Counters to remove everyone and start fresh.

👤
A third person accidentally joined

The app blocks a third counter from joining. If it happens due to a device issue, tap Clear All Counters on the name entry screen, then have only the two correct counters re-join.

Internal Controls Reminder
✅
These rules protect the integrity of the count
  • Always count independently — do not share your totals with the other counter before locking.
  • Do not change your quantity to match the other counter without physically recounting.
  • A counter cannot confirm their own check entries.
  • The offering cannot be submitted until both independent counts fully agree.
